A. Collision Avoidance
	Warn drivers if another vehicle is approaching at high speed.
	Alert if a vehicle suddenly brakes.

B. Intersection Safety
	Avoid crashes at junctions even when visibility is limited.

C. Lane-Change Assistance
	Check if a vehicle in the blind spot is approaching fast.

D. Forward Collision Warning
	If the vehicle ahead suddenly slows down.

E. Emergency Vehicle Notification
	Police, ambulance, and fire trucks send alerts (e.g., siren coming).

F. Cooperative Adaptive Cruise Control (CACC)
	Platooning of vehicles to improve fuel efficiency.

G. Hazard Information Sharing
	Slippery road ahead, potholes, fog, accident zones.
